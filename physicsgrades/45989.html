Model used on this page: overall = (0.90 × average(assessments)) + (0.10 × lesson_average) + constant_offset.
lesson_average and constant_offset are held fixed so the page starts at your official current grade.
This is a planning / visualization tool. It does not change your gradebook and may differ from the official calculation if categories, dropped items, or extra credit rules change.

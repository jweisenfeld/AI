**First — your DVR question answered:** It's right there in the list: **Title 490 — Workforce Training and Education Coordinating Board, *Also Vocational Rehabilitation*.** That "Also Vocational Rehabilitation" is DVR's WAC home. Pair it with RCW 74.29.

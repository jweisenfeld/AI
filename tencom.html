The Ten Commandments

1. You shall have no other gods before Me. ▶
Worship only God and do not put other things above Him.
2. You shall not make idols. ▶
Do not worship statues or images as gods.
3. You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain. ▶
Always respect God's name and speak it with honor.
4. Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy. ▶
Take a day of rest to honor God and refresh yourself.
5. Honor your father and your mother. ▶
Respect and obey your parents.
6. You shall not murder. ▶
Respect the life of every person.
7. You shall not commit adultery. ▶
Be faithful in marriage and relationships.
8. You shall not steal. ▶
Respect other people's property and do not take what isn't yours.
9. You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor. ▶
Always tell the truth and do not lie about others.
10. You shall not covet anything that belongs to your neighbor. ▶
Be happy with what you have and don’t envy others.
